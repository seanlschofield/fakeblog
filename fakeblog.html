About Sean

Sean has been photographing weddings and portraits since 2001. He went to school at Antonelli Institute in Erdenheim, PA where he got his associates degree, then continued on to Brooks Institute of Photography in Santa Barbara, CA where he got his bachelors in portraiture and photojournalism. Sean has since been running his own business and freelancing in the wedding and portrait industry. Recently, Sean became tired of running his own business and started disliking taking photographs, so he decided to take on a new career in coding with the goal of becoming a developer in the gaming industry.
